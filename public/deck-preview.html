Product Overview

One listing.  Many stories.

Lemma is a persona-aware video agent for property listings. Real estate agents and short-term rental hosts use Lemma to turn one upload into four cinematic films — each tuned to a different buyer. Embed one line of JS; every visitor sees the version made for them.

The Problem

One ad.
Every viewer.

Every property listing — for sale or for stay — ships one generic video that tries to speak to families, couples, investors, and remote workers all at once. Most viewers see content that has nothing to do with how they'd actually live in the space. Conversion leaks at the last mile.

The Opportunity

Same space.
Different story.

Two markets, one wedge: real estate agents fighting for buyer attention, and short-term rental hosts converting on their own sites — Hostfully, Lodgify, Squarespace, Wix. Both ship one generic video today. Both want the right one. Lemma is the agent that delivers it.
