## Hackathon Tracks

These tracks are meant to provide guidance and inspiration — they are not restrictive. You are free to explore ideas across tracks or build something completely different if that better fits your vision.

| # | Track | Description |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | **AI Video Agents (Vertical)** | AI agents that automate end-to-end workflows for specific industries using video — real estate walkthroughs, training content, personalized sales videos. |
| 2 | **AI Content Automation** | Pipelines that turn raw inputs (text, data, images) into ready-to-publish video content. Scalable, repeatable systems for marketing, e-commerce, media. |
| 3 | **Video DevTools** | Tools, SDKs, or infrastructure that help developers integrate video generation. Orchestration, APIs, multi-model systems. |
| 4 | **Physical AI + Simulation** | Use video generation to simulate environments or generate training data for robotics / autonomous agents. |
| 5 | **Open Track** | Don't like any of the above? Build anything you want — be creative and surprise us. |

## Final Demo Day — Presentation Guidelines

- **Selection:** Top teams notified by 2:00 PM (includes presentation order)
- **Pitch Format:** 3 minutes total per team. No Q&A during pitch.
- **Demo Requirement (Important):** Only the submitted video will be used. **No live demos allowed.**
- **Deck Handling:** All slides merged into one master deck
- **On-Stage Flow:** Teams called by name; present immediately when called
- **Attendance:** At least one team member must be present. No-show teams may be skipped.
